Traveling outside of the US is an experience that shapes me as a person. I feel that it is extremely important for people to travel if possible, as it gives you a stark reminder that you are just one little piece of a vast world. Traveling has helped humble me and open me to different ways of thinking that have been integral to my development as a person.
